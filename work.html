City Point Targówek Warsaw

Client: Peakside Capital Advisors
Execution period: 2023
Scope of work: general contracting
Location: Warsaw
Total area: 22,6 ha

Comprehensive works carried out for the purposes of the new City Point Targówek logistics park in Warsaw included the demolition of eight warehouse and office buildings along with the associated surfaces and networks, as well as cleaning up the site in an area of 22.6 ha. All demolishing works were carried out in accordance with the principles of sustainable development and the guidelines for BREEAM Excellent and LEED Gold certifications.

Student Depot, Warsaw

Client: Student Depot
Execution period: 2022-2023
Scope of work: general contracting
Location: Warsaw
Total area: 18,500 sqm

The Student Depot dormitory with a total area of 18.5 thousand sqm is located in Mokotów in Warsaw. It has from 5 to 13 above-ground storeys and an underground storey with a garage. The building is comprised of 543 premium residential units, including single, double and 4-person rooms with areas ranging from 16 to 79.8 sqm.

Designer Outlet Warszawa, Piaseczno

Client: ROS Retail Outlet Shopping
Execution period: 2021-2022
Scope of work: execution design, general contracting
Location: Piaseczno (n. Warsaw)
Total area: 37,771 m²

Designer Outlet Warsaw after the extension has a modern and advanced BMS system controlling, among others, lighting, air-conditioning, media consumption and monitoring.The extension and redevelopment of the commercial and service facility Designer Outlet Warsaw took place in two stages.
Stage I consisted of preparing the site for the extension.Stage II consisted of extending the single-storey commercial and service facility by an additional 7,106 m².

Panattoni Park Pabianice, Neonet

Client: Panattoni
Execution period: 2021-2022
Scope of work: design & build
Location: Pabianice
Total area: 44 000 sqm

The modern and environment-friendly logistics and warehouse facility with an area of 44,000 sqm – part of Panattoni Park Pabianice – is adapted to the needs of its tenant, i.e. Neonet – a chain of stores selling household appliances, electronics and IT equipment. Apart from the warehouse part, the facility also has a two-storey office and staff part (1,110 sqm) and an internal office building on the premises of the hall (210 sqm). During the design and construction works, Kajima Poland supervised the preparation of the facility for obtaining a BREEAM certificate with an Excellent rating. The entire facility has numerous solutions aimed at reducing energy and water consumption as well as enhancing well-being.

Hines, Hipark Rzepin

Client: Hines Polska
Execution period: 2022
Scope of work: design & build, BREEAM
Location: Rzepin
Total area: 52 000 sqm

Hipark Rzepin is a modern class A logistics center with a total area of 52,000 sqm. The entire facility has been designed and implemented to comply with the requirements of modern logistics. It is also environmentally friendly and comfortable to use, which is confirmed by the BREEAM certificate at the Very Good level.
