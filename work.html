City Point Targówek Warsaw

Client: Peakside Capital Advisors
Execution period: 2023
Scope of work: general contracting
Location: Warsaw
Total area: 22,6 ha

Comprehensive works carried out for the purposes of the new City Point Targówek logistics park in Warsaw included the demolition of eight warehouse and office buildings along with the associated surfaces and networks, as well as cleaning up the site in an area of 22.6 ha. All demolishing works were carried out in accordance with the principles of sustainable development and the guidelines for BREEAM Excellent and LEED Gold certifications.

Student Depot, Warsaw

Client: Student Depot
Execution period: 2022-2023
Scope of work: general contracting
Location: Warsaw
Total area: 18,500 sqm

The Student Depot dormitory with a total area of 18.5 thousand sqm is located in Mokotów in Warsaw. It has from 5 to 13 above-ground storeys and an underground storey with a garage. The building is comprised of 543 premium residential units, including single, double and 4-person rooms with areas ranging from 16 to 79.8 sqm.
