Projects I have worked on...

City Point Targówek Warsaw

Client: Peakside Capital Advisors
Execution period: 2023
Scope of work: general contracting
Location: Warsaw
Total area: 22,6 ha

Extensive work undertaken for the new City Point Targówek logistics park in Warsaw involved tearing down eight warehouse and office structures, along with the related surfaces and utilities, and clearing an area of 22.6 hectares. All demolition activities were conducted in line with sustainable development principles and adhered to the standards for BREEAM Excellent and LEED Gold certifications.

Student Depot, Warsaw

Client: Student Depot
Execution period: 2022-2023
Scope of work: general contracting
Location: Warsaw
Total area: 18,500 sqm

The Student Depot dormitory, covering a total area of 18,500 square meters, is situated in Mokotów, Warsaw. The building ranges from 5 to 13 floors above ground and includes an underground level with a garage. It features 543 high-quality residential units, consisting of single, double, and 4-person rooms, with sizes between 16 and 79.8 square meters.

Designer Outlet Warszawa, Piaseczno

Client: ROS Retail Outlet Shopping
Execution period: 2021-2022
Scope of work: execution design, general contracting
Location: Piaseczno (n. Warsaw)
Total area: 37,771 m²

After its expansion, Designer Outlet Warsaw is now equipped with a cutting-edge Building Management System (BMS) that manages various functions such as lighting, air-conditioning, energy consumption, and monitoring. The project to extend and renovate the Designer Outlet Warsaw was completed in two phases. The first phase involved site preparation for the extension. The second phase included adding 7,106 square meters to the single-storey commercial and service facility.

Panattoni Park Pabianice, Neonet

Client: Panattoni
Execution period: 2021-2022
Scope of work: design & build
Location: Pabianice
Total area: 44 000 sqm

Part of Panattoni Park Pabianice, a 44,000 sqm modern and eco-friendly logistics and warehouse facility, is tailored to Neonet, a retail chain for appliances, electronics, and IT equipment. The facility includes a two-storey office and staff area (1,110 sqm) and an internal office building (210 sqm). Kajima Poland oversaw its design and construction, ensuring it met the criteria for an Excellent BREEAM certification. The facility incorporates various solutions to reduce energy and water usage and improve overall well-being.

Hines, Hipark Rzepin

Client: Hines Polska
Execution period: 2022
Scope of work: design & build, BREEAM
Location: Rzepin
Total area: 52 000 sqm

Hines Park Rzepin is a cutting-edge Class A logistics hub encompassing 52,000 square meters. The facility is meticulously designed to meet the demands of contemporary logistics operations. Additionally, it emphasizes sustainability and user comfort, as reflected in its BREEAM "Very Good" certification, attesting to its eco-friendly and efficient design.
